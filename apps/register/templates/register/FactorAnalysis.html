(d) The age between 50 to 90, the proportion of people reach 50k annul income increase.
(e) However, the amount people decrease in age range between 50 to 90

conclusion:
(1) Age between 20 to 40 is the important period to accumulate wealth
(2) Age above 35, most people enter the golden period to have a certain amount of wealth.
(3) As people get older, it becomes harder to keep accumulate wealth.
